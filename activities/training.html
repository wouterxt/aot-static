Training and development programs for grassroots forms of innovation are specific to a particular context. It needs to address the whole person. Our expertise is how to build a successful program.

Whether in a neighboorhood context, in (local) politics or in any organizational setting, training and development program improve participantsâ€™ motivation and skill level. The benefits include better personal and social skills, a goal-oriented way of operating, a higher level of strategic thinking and more independence. No matter how motivated people are to change something in their world there is always room for growth. Engaging in neighbourhood and other types of activism has its own particular pitfalls. There is great benefit by exploring these issues both with your colleagues and with others who might be active in a different field.
